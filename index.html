About

My name is Chloe and I’m a London-based personal trainer, specializing in wellness and nutrition.

Having worked in PR since graduating university, in 2017 I qualified as a personal trainer and nutritionist. I decided to trade in the dresses for gym gear full time and I haven’t looked back since. I understand that fitting in exercise around full-time work and commitments can sometimes seem impossible. My method is to create challenging and enjoyable workouts for people of all levels that fit in around your day-to-day life.


Because getting fit shouldn’t be about sacrifice.

Session Info

Each workout is tailored to the individual and based on many disciplines. My approach utilizes the dynamics of bodyweight training, combined with the principles of yoga and resistance training. Services include HIIT, strength training, weight loss, functional training, circuits and boxing.

As a qualified nutritionist, I take a holistic approach to your training and will work with you to put together a training and nutrition plan based on your goals.
